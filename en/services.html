Frequently asked questions about services

How to understand which service I need?

If you want a quick check - start with SiteMoney Scan for €149: within 10-15 minutes, you will receive a map of critical losses. If you need a complete analysis of the site as a sales system - choose Money Leak Audit. For constant monitoring, it will do. subscription.

In what format does the diagnostic result come?

The result is automatically generated in Google Sheets (structured data) and duplicated in Telegram (quick access). For SiteMoney Scan, a PDF report with priorities is additionally generated. All automation is built on n8n - without manual work.

How does your diagnosis differ from free SEO checks?

Free services show technical metrics (speed, meta tags). We analyze the site as a sales system: offer, persuasion structure, logic of conversion paths, trust triggers. This is the difference between "you have 85 on PageSpeed" and "here is where you are losing clients and why."

How quickly will I get results?

SiteMoney Scan - PDF report within 24 hours. Money Leak Audit - complete analysis in 5-7 business days. Express data from automatic scanning arrives in Telegram immediately after analysis.
