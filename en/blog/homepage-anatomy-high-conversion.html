Above-the-fold and site structure

Homepage: anatomy of high conversion

March 23, 2026 5 min
TL;DR: Want to increase your website conversion? Start with the above-the-fold. Learn how site structure and navigation impact users and how to optimize your landing page for maximum results.

Why the above-the-fold matters

The above-the-fold is the first impression of your website. Research shows you have only 5-7 seconds to engage a visitor. If they cannot find the right information or the site looks unappealing, bounce rate increases.

How to design the above-the-fold right

  • Clear value proposition: Show what you offer and why it matters.
  • Attractive design: Aesthetics matter. Use quality images and readable fonts.
  • Call to action (CTA): Place it where it is easy to find.

Site structure: the foundation of success

Site structure helps users quickly find the right information. A well-planned structure improves user experience and boosts conversion.

Key elements of site structure:

  1. Navigation menu: Must be intuitive and accessible.
  2. Categories and subcategories: Organize content logically.
  3. Sitemaps: Simplify indexing for search engines.

Navigation: key to user retention

Poor navigation leads to high bounce rates. Make sure users can easily move through your site.

Tips for better navigation:

  • Clear labels: Use words users expect to see.
  • Mobile-friendly: Ensure navigation works on all devices.
  • Minimal clicks: Users should reach any page easily.

Landing page: how to optimize for conversions

A landing page is where users arrive most often. It must be focused on a single goal.

What makes a successful landing page?

  1. Single focus: Do not overwhelm users with information.
  2. Social proof: Show testimonials or case studies.
  3. Testing: Continuously improve the landing page based on data.

Case studies and success examples

Many successful companies like Amazon and Airbnb pay special attention to the above-the-fold and site structure. This helped them achieve high conversion and retention rates.

Frequently asked questions

Why is the above-the-fold so important?

First impressions form in seconds. If the above-the-fold does not capture attention, the user may leave.

How to improve site structure?

Optimize navigation, use clear labels, and implement sitemaps for better indexing.

What makes a good call to action?

A clear, visible element that encourages action - like subscribing or buying.

How does a landing page affect conversions?

A landing page focuses on one goal, simplifying user decisions and boosting conversion.

Why test your landing page?

Testing reveals weak points and improves conversion based on real data.

Key takeaways

  • The above-the-fold forms first impressions in 5-7 seconds.
  • Clear site structure improves user experience.
  • Intuitive navigation reduces bounce rates.
  • Landing pages need a single focus and social proof.
  • Continuous landing page testing improves conversions.
