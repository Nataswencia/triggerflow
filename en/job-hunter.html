Frequently asked questions

How do I get started?

Open @JobHunter_triggerflow_bot in Telegram, tap /start, choose your language, and fill out your profile. In 2 minutes the bot will start searching for jobs.

Where do the job listings come from?

From 4 sources: Google Jobs (aggregator), Welcome to the Jungle (top platform in France), Arbeitnow (Europe), and RemoteOK (remote). The bot checks all of them every day at 09:00.

What is AI scoring?

GPT-4o-mini compares each job with your profile (skills, experience, salary, location) and assigns a score from 0 to 100%. You only receive relevant positions.

Is it really free?

Yes. Full daily search, AI scoring, and Telegram notifications are free. A PRO version with advanced features will be available later.

Can I change my profile after signing up?

Yes. The /profile command lets you update your profile at any time. The /language command lets you switch the interface language.

Does the bot work for all countries?

The sources cover France, Germany, the Netherlands, Belgium, Spain, and remote positions worldwide. You specify your target country during sign-up.
