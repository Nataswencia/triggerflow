Questions about technologies

Why does TriggerFlow use n8n instead of Zapier?

n8n supports complex AI chains with GPT-4o, custom analysis logic, and self-hosted deployment for data privacy. Zapier is limited to linear scenarios and does not support deep multi-level analysis. For website diagnostics, a platform is needed that can handle dozens of parameters in one chain - n8n does this.

How does the integration with Telegram work?

You send the website URL to the bot @TriggerFlow_analyse_bot in Telegram. The AI system on n8n analyzes the site based on 50+ parameters and sends the result back to Telegram: a structured report with priorities. For express analysis - in 24 hours, for full audit - in 5-7 days

Why is Google Sheets needed in diagnostics?

Google Sheets stores all analysis data in a structured format: metrics, errors, priorities. This allows tracking the dynamics of changes between audits, working with data in a team, and seeing progress. subscription n8n automatically compares current data with previous ones and shows what has improved and what needs attention.

Is it safe to transmit site data through the bot?

Yes. n8n operates on a private server - data is not shared with third parties. Only publicly available pages of the website are analyzed, without access to the admin panel or personal data. This is a fundamental difference from cloud solutions like Zapier, where data passes through third-party servers.
