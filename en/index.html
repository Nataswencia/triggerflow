Frequently asked questions

What is TriggerFlow and how does it differ from a regular SEO agency?

TriggerFlow is a growth diagnostics system, not an SEO agency. We do not sell design, SEO, or advertising. We identify specific points where your site is losing profit and provide an action plan with priorities. You gain clarity: what to do, in what order, and why.

How does the free AI website analysis work?

Enter your website URL in the form on our homepage. The AI agent will analyze the site in 2 minutes across 50+ parameters: technical condition, SEO, content, structure. You will receive the results by email within 24 hours.

Who are TriggerFlow's services suitable for?

For entrepreneurs, experts, and growing projects. If you have a website, have traffic, but lack stable sales growth - our diagnostics will show exactly where the problem lies and what to do first.

Where is the best place to start?

Start with a free AI diagnostics on our website or with the SiteMoney Scan from 79€. Light - AI analysis + PDF, PRO (149€) - expert review with screenshots.

In what format will you receive the result?

Depending on the chosen service: PDF report with priorities, screenshots with comments, Google Sheets with analysis data. All without technical jargon - clear for entrepreneurs.

What technologies does TriggerFlow use?

Diagnostics are based on n8n - an open-source automation platform that provides complete control and flexibility. Results are delivered by email and saved in Google Sheets. AI agents based on GPT-4o analyze the site according to 50+ parameters automatically.

How does the paid analysis differ from the free one?

The free AI diagnostics analyzes one page and gives general recommendations in 2 minutes - enough to understand the scale of problems. SiteMoney Scan from 79€ checks the entire site, provides a PDF report with action priorities, competitive analysis, and ROI assessment. How does automated diagnostics differ from manual audit?

A manual audit depends on the attentiveness of the specialist and takes days. Our system on n8n checks the site according to a single standard, does not miss a single parameter, and delivers results in minutes. At the same time, final recommendations are prepared by an expert - automation helps, but does not replace experience.
