Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: March 2026

TriggerFlow respects your right to control your personal data. You can request the deletion of any data we hold about you at any time.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, send an email to:

Email: nataswencia87@gmail.com

Subject: Data Deletion Request

In your email, please include:

  1. Your full name
  2. The email address associated with your account
  3. Your Instagram username (if applicable)
  4. A description of what data you would like deleted

What Happens Next

  • We will acknowledge your request within 48 hours.
  • Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of receiving your request.
  • You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

What Data Gets Deleted

Upon request, we will delete:

  • Your account information and profile data
  • Instagram comments and messages processed through our service
  • Any automation rules or configurations you created
  • Analytics and usage data associated with your account

Exceptions

We may retain certain data if required by law (e.g., invoicing records for tax purposes). In such cases, the data will be kept only for the legally required period and will not be used for any other purpose.
